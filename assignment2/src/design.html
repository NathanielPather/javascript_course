Text Rules

Text is kept as simple as possible.
Text kept in its own portion of the grid.
Text is further grouped in its own table.

Contrast

Elements within the page were measured with the squint test.
Elements deemed to be more important than others were made darker than unimportant elements.
This directs the users focus to more important parts of the website.

Proximity

Grouping data that belongs together.

Interactivity

Important elements are made to be and look interactable.
This increasing learning support relying on interaction rather than reading, seeing, and hearing.
